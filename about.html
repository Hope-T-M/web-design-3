About Fiora

Fiora Flowers was founded in 2017 by floral artist and shopkeeper Hope Kelley. With a lifelong passion for horticulture and design, Hope opened her dream neighborhood floral boutique to share beauty with her community. Nestled in the heart of downtown, our charming storefront is inspired by Hope's philosophy of using nature's beauty to brighten each day. Stepping inside is like entering a blooming oasis, with fragrant stems and greenery artistically arranged throughout. For every order, large or small, Hope selects only the freshest seasonal blooms. With innovative arrangements and her signature artistic style, she then transforms each bouquet, basket, and installation into a one-of-a-kind floral gift. In addition to walk-in sales, Fiora Flowers offers full-service event floral design, from centerpieces to garlands. We pride ourselves on impeccable and prompt delivery for all celebrations - from weddings and anniversaries, to new baby and get-well bouquets. At our boutique, you'll find not only artfully crafted floral designs, but also a friendly staff dedicated to customer satisfaction. Our mission is to spread joy through flowers, whether you're looking to congratulate, console or simply treat yourself. Stop by to experience the warmth of Fiora Flowers for yourself!
